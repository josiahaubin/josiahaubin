Who Am I?

Greetings, my name is Josiah Aubin. I am a software developer who loves all things coding. I developed a love for coding after my first coding class in college. After a few more classes that required coding and getting lost in it for hours on end, I realized that a career as a developer was for me. I attended Boise CodeWorks to futher my coding skill and to gain the knowledge needed to perform at my best in the industry.

If I am not spending my time behind a computer writing lines of code you can find me:

  • Completing main missions (after all the side missions of course), capturing objectives, holding control points, or pushing payloads in some of my favorite videogames.
  • Spending time outdoors camping, fishing, and hunting.
  • Getting excerise by lifting weights or playing basketball.
  • Laughing with friends.
  • Enjoying time with my family.
